### Why become a member of a Blockchain Consortium?

1. Commercial Solutions: These groups are focused upon to create a framework aimed at Industry Addressing Challenges.

2. Technology Development: Most consortia aim to furnish reusable platforms conforming to consensus-based protocols, thereby periodically facilitating interoperability across the industry.

Take R3 as an instance; it epitomizes the quintessential union between business and technology, exemplifying how disparate aims can operate seamlessly in one consortium.

### The Road Ahead

Nevertheless, this may not be that easily attained. As these organizations grow, it becomes increasingly difficult for them to accept new members and ease their roles into old IT systems. Following the progress made by other companies like R3 could give crucial indications on overcoming structural barriers.

### Takeaways for Your Organization

- Start Early: Remember that preliminary discussions with potential consortium partners should commence soon; cooperation takes time.

- Stay Afloat: Be observant of ways that other consortia have done things to begin assessing which worked and which did not.

- Cultivate Trust: Encourage open conversation among members to understand where accountability is imposed and find areas of overlap.
